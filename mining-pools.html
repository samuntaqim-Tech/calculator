Mining Pool Payout Methods

PPS (Pay Per Share)

Fixed payment for each share submitted. Most stable income but higher fees.

PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares)

Payment based on last N shares. Better long-term profits, lower fees.

FPPS (Full Pay Per Share)

PPS + transaction fees. Best of both worlds, most profitable currently.

Solo Mining

Mine alone, keep 100% of block reward. High risk, high reward.

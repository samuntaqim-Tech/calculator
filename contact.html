Frequently Asked Questions

How often is mining profitability data updated? +

Our mining profitability data is updated every 5 minutes with real-time cryptocurrency prices and network difficulty. Hardware specifications and prices are reviewed and updated daily.

Can I suggest new mining hardware to be added? +

Yes! We welcome suggestions for new mining hardware. Please use the contact form above with the manufacturer, model, and specifications of the hardware you'd like us to add to our database.

Do you provide investment advice? +

We provide mining profitability calculations and hardware comparisons, but we do not offer specific investment advice. All mining investments carry risk, and you should conduct your own research before investing.

How can I report incorrect data? +

If you find any incorrect data on our website, please use the contact form and select "Data Correction" as the subject. Include the specific page and the correction needed, and we'll review it promptly.

Are you affiliated with any mining hardware manufacturers? +

We maintain independence from hardware manufacturers to provide unbiased comparisons. Some manufacturers may be advertising partners, but this does not influence our rankings or reviews.

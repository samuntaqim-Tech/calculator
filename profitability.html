Profitability Calculation Tips

⚡ Electricity Costs

Electricity is the largest ongoing cost for mining. Rates vary from $0.05/kWh (cheap) to $0.30/kWh (expensive).

📈 Network Difficulty

Mining difficulty adjusts regularly. Higher difficulty means lower rewards for the same hashpower.

💰 Hardware Depreciation

ASIC miners lose value quickly as newer, more efficient models are released.

🌡️ Cooling & Maintenance

Factor in cooling costs and regular maintenance for accurate profitability calculations.
